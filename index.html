About The Hosts

Graham

Graham spends most of his time being delightfully middle class. He doesn't believe badgers are real and thinks Mad Max: Fury Road is boring. His faithful hound Bowie is often heard snoring in the background.

Ian

Ian is often found lamenting the state of Welsh rugby and trying to watch the entire back catalogue of Nicolas Cage films and Chicago Fire episodes. He likes long walks in the park and Toby Jones once accidentally hit him with a suitcase.
